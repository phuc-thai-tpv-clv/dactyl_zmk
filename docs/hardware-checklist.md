# Hardware Checklist

## Firmware Mapping

- Confirm actual switch count per half
- Confirm diode direction
- Confirm row and column GPIO choices on each `nice!nano`
- Confirm whether encoder, display, or pointing device will be added later

## Case Design

- Confirm column stagger targets
- Confirm thumb cluster angle and count
- Confirm tenting target
- Confirm wall thickness
- Confirm controller tray or standoff approach
- Confirm battery dimensions
- Confirm switch plate thickness and tolerance for your printer

## Manufacturing

- Print a small fit test for switch cutouts first
- Test controller and battery fit before printing the full shell
- Plan threaded inserts if you want a serviceable enclosure
